    Job Readiness

    Sprint 4 was all about job readiness. we had two features which are the pre-apply and post apply. The user stories for the pre apply is basically everything we need to do before applying for a job which are resume, cover letter and social media platforms(LinkedIn, Monster, Indeed etc). For the post apply user stories we did our project stories like what did we actually do? which brings us to the 5W1H(When, Where, What, Why, Who and How). We also did our Artificial Intelligent(AI) which aid our application process.
